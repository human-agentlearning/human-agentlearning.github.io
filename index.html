About Me

I am currently an Associate Professor at the Department of Computer Science, University of Warwick, UK. My main research area is Artificial Intelligence. I am interested in building complex learning systems where humans and agents have to either: (i) collaborate to deal with complex real-world problems; or (ii) to strategically act against each other. Given this, I mainly focus on the following topics: online learning/decision making, game theory, incentive engineering, and multi-agent systems.
